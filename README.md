# Renting_Platform

## Members and the project
- Members
  - Renat Magsumov
  - Carl-Eric Sepp
- Project <br>
The aim is to create a web platform to rent out items. Similar to Facebook Marketplace- people post items that they want to rent out. The person renting an item out gets a daily or fixed amount and the person who is renting an item gets the item(s) for a fixed amount of time.

## Unique value offering
There are multiple renting platforms, yet they are very specialized and the one renting items out is a company. As there really isn't any platform to rent out _anything_, at least in Estonia- it fills a void for people who need something, yet don't want to buy it outright.

## Target group
- Age: 25-40
- People with a little bit of technical knowledge

## Communication and visual style

## Call to actions
